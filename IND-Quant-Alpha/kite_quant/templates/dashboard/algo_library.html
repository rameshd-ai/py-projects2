label What the tags mean

Market type

  • TREND — Best when price moves in one direction (momentum, breakouts).
  • RANGE — Best when price oscillates (mean reversion, fade).
  • VOLATILE — For expansion/breakout in choppy or explosive moves.
  • EVENT — News or catalyst-driven (earnings, results).
  • OPTIONS — Options-specific (IV, straddle, gamma).
  • ADVANCED — AI/meta logic (regime, MTF, liquidity, sizing).

Risk level

  • LOW — Lower capital at risk; smaller moves, tighter stops.
  • MEDIUM — Moderate risk and position size; balanced reward.
  • HIGH — Higher risk; larger moves or options decay; use strict sizing.

Good for

  • STOCK — Suited for equity / stock F&O.
  • INDEX — Suited for index F&O (Nifty, Bank Nifty).
  • OPTIONS — Suited for options (CE/PE, straddles, IV plays).
