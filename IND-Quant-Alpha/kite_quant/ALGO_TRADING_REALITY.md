## Why AI Can't Guarantee Profits (Technical Reality)

### 1. AI Doesn't Have a Crystal Ball

**What AI Actually Does:**
```python
# AI looks at past data
past_data = last_10_candles
pattern = ai.analyze(past_data)

# AI makes educated guess
if pattern == "bullish_momentum":
    recommendation = "Use Momentum Breakout strategy"
    
# But market can STILL reverse!

```

**AI can't predict:**
- Sudden news events (RBI announcement, global crisis)
- Large institutional orders
- Market manipulation
- Random noise in price movement

### 2. The Efficient Market Hypothesis

**Why consistent 60% daily returns are impossible:**

If someone truly had an algorithm that made 60%/day:
1. They would use ₹10 lakh → ₹6 crore in 30 days
2. They would use ₹1 crore → ₹600 crore in 30 days
3. They would be the richest person in the world in 6 months

**Why this doesn't happen:**
- As you make profit, your edge decreases
- Market adapts to patterns
- Slippage and liquidity issues at scale
- Regulatory limits

### 3. Market Makers Would Stop You

If you consistently extracted money, exchanges/brokers would:
- Flag your account
- Add restrictions
- Increase margin requirements
- Ban your strategies

   - Someone still loses
